Caesar Salad

Description

A healthy, quick salad that is a great side or starter for any meal. Goes great with our lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 head Romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup shaved Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup croutons

Directions

  1. Wash and chop Romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix lettuce, dressing, and cheese together in a bowl.
  3. Top with cheese and croutons and serve.
